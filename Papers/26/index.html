Multi-agent control of an industry 4.0 cyber-physical assembly cell*’

Authors: M. Gaham, M. Kadri, A. Maoudj, A. Bendjelloul, A. Kouider, B. Mihoubi, K. Tebani & B. Bouzouia

Published:  |  Journal: 1st National Conference on Applied Computing and Smart Technologies. Ecole Supérieure en Informatique, ,

Abstract

in this paper we present a multi-agent architecture for the modeling and implementation of an Industry 4.0 robotic assembly system. The proposal is motivated by the adequation of the agent concept to the development of cyber-physical distributed control systems. Besides, the paper highlight the experimental deployment of the JADE based developed control architecture using an edge device on a real assembly cell.
